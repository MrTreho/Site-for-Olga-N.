About the author

Hi, my name is Olga. I create art. I am a very sensual person and cannot imagine my life without art, inspiration, and creativity. When I am in the process of painting a new painting, the world around stops the world around me stops, and I plunge into some other universe. My canvases are about feelings. Don't seek to define the sense. Just watch and enjoy. My fundamental mission is to evoke feelings and emotions in you because they fill our lives.
